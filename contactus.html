Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge for counselling?

Yes, there is a counselling fee involved. It covers the time and expertise of our experienced advisors to ensure you receive the best support.

Can I apply without IELTS?

Yes, you can apply without IELTS in some cases. Many universities accept alternatives like medium of instruction letters, other English tests, or may offer internal assessments. It depends on the country and institution.

How early should I apply before intake?

It's best to apply at least 6 to 12 months before your desired intake. This gives you enough time for admission, visa processing, and other formalities.
