Welcome to PS Educons Recruits

Your trusted partner in making your study abroad dream a reality. Founded by Parthiban Mohana (also known as Parthi Reddy, a successful YouTuber) and his better half, Dr. Devishree.M.D, we specialize in helping students from Tamil Nadu and all over India pursue higher education in top destinations like the UK, Canada, USA, and more.

Our Story

Our consultancy was born from real-life experience. When Parthiban moved to the UK for his studies, he faced the exact challenges that thousands of students encounter — confusing processes, lack of trustworthy guidance, and cultural uncertainty. With that in mind, Parthiban and Dr. Devishree decided to build a consultancy that’s honest, relatable, and student-focused.

Our Mission

To provide transparent, ethical, and personalized support to every student who dreams of studying abroad. From selecting the right course to visa guidance, accommodation, and post-arrival support — we’re with you at every step.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted study abroad consultancy in South India, known for our values, results, and student-first approach. We want to empower every student from Tier 2 & Tier 3 towns with the tools to succeed globally.
